About Us

The Wild Circus company was created in 2014 by Anna Stépanoff and Romain Coeur.

Ours circuses are présent all over Europe especially in France. We have 19 sites to welcome young and old during extraordinary shows.

Shows are different from the usual because we want to put the public at the center of the performances so that they are the main actor.

With our raffles you may have the chance to come on stage to help the clowns in their comedies, lend a hand to the tiger trainer or join the team of acrobats,
...and more surprises.
